Most like writing about positive things first so here goes. What you first need to hear is that my personality is deeply rooted in logical and critical thinking. Seeing the bigger picture, finding the most efficient solution and the way to get there has always been my strong point. Hand to hand with this is my capability to stay within schedule. Last time I was late going somewhere or with a schoolwork was probably during elementary school (so long ago don’t even remember what). My personality also consists of wanting to learn new things with an open mind and being unbiased. As for social skills I’m friendly and an excellent listener, but a stereotypical finnish person when it comes to opening my mouth. And the more unflattering things include sometimes taking the easy way out, not saying what should be said and all round social aptitude which sometimes comes off as cold.

What I want to improve

  • Communicational skills
  • Teamwork skills
  • More realistic risk analysis (a falling meteor isn’t something that I need to account for)
  • Courage to go forth
