Early

Age 22, always lived in Espoo, living with family of three, athletic history which includes track and field and later gymnastics, both for several years and medals. For school I went to the local elementary and from there graduated to Olari’s secondary school. Other mentionable things include travelling a lot with my family across Europe, having visited half of the countries and that I’ve considered my childhood to be a good experience with it’s ups and (mostly) downs.

Later to now

From Olari’s secondary school I managed to get high grades which enabled me to contintue in the same building: Olari’s upper secondary school. At this point point I picked up skiing to replace my other sports. After graduating I spent two years searching my road during which I tried many different paths. In the winter 2018 I decided to apply to Laurea university of applied sciences, line being Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. A few weeks later I received information that I’ve been accepted. Currently I am studying there, happy with my choice.
